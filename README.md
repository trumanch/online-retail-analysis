# Online Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyses transactional retail data to identify revenue trends, customer behaviour, product performance, and country-level sales patterns.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
Online Retail transactional dataset containing order, product, customer, and country-level information.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value

## Analysis Performed
- Monthly revenue trend
- Top products by revenue
- Top customers by revenue
- Revenue by country
- Repeat customer analysis

## Key Findings
- Revenue varied significantly across months.
- A small number of products drove a large share of sales.
- Repeat customers were an important source of revenue.
- The UK was the strongest-performing country in the dataset.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products to maximise revenue.
- Develop retention strategies targeting high-value and repeat customers.
- Investigate low-performing products to optimise inventory and pricing.
- Explore expansion opportunities in high-performing regions beyond the UK.
