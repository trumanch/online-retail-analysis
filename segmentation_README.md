# Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis. The goal is to identify different groups of customers based on their purchasing behaviour and provide insights to support business decision-making.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
Online Retail transactional dataset containing customer purchases, product details, and order information.

## Methodology

### RFM Metrics
- **Recency (R):** Days since last purchase
- **Frequency (F):** Number of unique purchases
- **Monetary (M):** Total spending by customer

Customers were grouped into segments based on these metrics.

## Segments Identified
- **VIP Customers:** High frequency and recent purchases
- **Loyal Customers:** Frequent buyers with consistent activity
- **New Customers:** Recently acquired customers
- **At Risk Customers:** Customers who haven’t purchased recently

## Key Findings
- VIP customers generate the highest share of total revenue.
- Loyal customers provide consistent revenue streams.
- At-risk customers represent an opportunity for re-engagement.
- New customers show potential for future growth.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus retention strategies on high-value (VIP and Loyal) customers.
- Re-engage at-risk customers through targeted marketing campaigns.
- Convert new customers into repeat buyers through onboarding incentives.
- Personalise offers based on customer segment behaviour.
